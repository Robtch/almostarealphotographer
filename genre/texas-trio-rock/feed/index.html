Texas Trio Rock – 準写真家 https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/ Fri, 24 Jul 2026 16:01:53 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=7.0.2 Billy F Gibbons https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/billy-gibbons/ Sat, 17 Jun 2023 08:25:00 +0000 https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/?p=205 A dream came true at Copenhell.

Billy Gibbons was one of my childhood heroes growing up and still is. He was a huge influence during my own short-lived music career.

For me, ZZ Top was one of the greatest trios ever. It was the unmistakable blues rock, the beards and those riffs that spoke directly to me.

Seeing Billy Gibbons on stage all these years later felt a little surreal. Some artists have been part of your life for so long that finally seeing them live becomes more than just another concert.

He entered. the main stage at Copenhell with his side project The BFG’s and served up some classics like “Sharp Dressed Man”, “Gimme All Your Lovin'”, “La Grange”, and “Beer Drinkers & Hell Raisers” (sourced from the Copenhell Setlist on Setlist.fm)

A moment I’ll always remember.
