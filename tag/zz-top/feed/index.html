ZZ Top – 準写真家 https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/ Mon, 20 Jul 2026 19:00:57 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=7.0.2 Billy Gibbons https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/billy-gibbons/ Tue, 13 May 2025 08:25:48 +0000 https://robtch.github.io/almostarealphotographer/?p=205 A dream came true at Copenhell.

Billy Gibbons was one of my childhood and teenage heroes growing up, and a huge influence during my own short-lived music career.

For me, ZZ Top was one of the greatest trios ever. It was the unmistakable blues rock, the beards and those riffs that spoke directly to me.

Seeing Billy Gibbons on stage all these years later felt a little surreal. Some artists have been part of your life for so long that finally seeing them live becomes more than just another concert.

A moment I’ll always remember.
